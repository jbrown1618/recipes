\recipe{
    Honey Balsamic Brussels Sprouts
}{
    \href{https://keviniscooking.com/roasted-brussels-sprouts-balsamic-vinegar-honey/}{Kevin is Cooking}
}{
    \Ingredients{
        \item 1 \half lbs fresh brussels sprouts
        \item 3 \tablespoons olive oil
        \item salt and pepper to taste
        \item 2 \tablespoons balsamic vinegar
        \item 2 \tablespoons honey
    }
}{
    \Directions{
        \item Preheat oven to 425 degrees F.
        \item Line a baking sheet with aluminum foil or use a Silpat silicone baking mat.
        \item Trim off the outer, dry leaves, cut the bottom off and slice sprouts lengthwise.
        \item In a large bowl, toss brussels sprouts with 2 \tablespoons of olive oil, kosher salt and freshly
              cracked black pepper to coat thoroughly.
        \item Transfer the brussels sprouts to baking sheet and roast until tender and caramelized, about 20 minutes.
        \item Place brussels sprouts back in bowl. Add remaining \tablespoon olive oil, balsamic vinegar and honey and
              toss to coat evenly.  Taste and season with kosher salt if necessary and serve.

    }
}
